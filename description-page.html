Description

Welcome to Emirates Park and Gardens Phase 2, a continuation of our prestigious "The Destination Estate". Our Emirates Park & Gardens (Ocean View), like its other counterpart, is located in Badary, Lagos; a few minutes from Badagry Heritage Museum.

Estates and other Landmarks within the neighbourhood include the First Story Building, the Badagry Slave and Black History Museum, Mobee Palace, and a host of many Historical Sites. This exclusive community offers a tranquil and luxurious lifestyle surrounded by breathtaking views.

Furthermore, Emirates Park and Gardens Phase 2 is strategically situated just a few minutes away from the renowned Badagry Heritage Museum.

Emirates Park & Gardens (Ocean View) boasts a prime location amidst a wealth of historical landmarks, including the First Story Building, the Badagry Slave and Black History Museum, and Mobee Palace. This community provides an unparalleled lifestyle experience, combining tranquility with easy access to these remarkable sites.

Designed for those who seek the perfect balance between comfort and elegance, Emirates Park and Gardens Phase 2 is an ideal choice for discerning homeowners and investors alike.
